A copy of the final mark sheets of 10 and 10+2 exams with originals for verification must be submitted to the Admission Office of the University at the time of reporting. In case of candidates appearing in the 12th Board examination in the year 2022, self-attested copies of the mark sheets with originals must be submitted on or before 31st October, 2022 for verification.

Post commencement Refund policy of BGU is guided by UGC guidelines.
