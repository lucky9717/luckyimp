B. SELECTION CRITERIA: The final selection criteria is based on following multiple factors
1. Marks obtained in S.S.C (10th), H.S.C (12th) and each semester/year of bachelor’s degree
2. Post-Graduation Degree (if any)
3. Entrance Test Score
4. Work Experience (if any)
5. Performance in the Micro Presentation and Personal Interview
C. ACCEPTANCE OF ADMISSION OFFER AND FEES PAYMENT:
1. Immediately after the completion of Micro Presentation and Personal Interview, you may be offered provisional admission to one or more programmes based on your overall performance in the selection process.
2. Should you choose to accept the Provisional Offer of Admission, you must submit a demand draft of ₹ 25,000/- which represent part-I of the programme fees along with offer acceptance undertaking. You will then be given a confirmation of admission as a receipt towards the initial fee payment.
3. Should you choose not to accept the Provisional Offer of Admission, you are under no obligation to make any payment. Please note that after such a time, the Provisional Offer of Admission may be revoked.
4. Once you have confirmed your acceptance of admission by paying the Part-1 of tuition fees, you are required to pay the remaining fees on or before the date specified in the Provisional Offer of Admission Letter. In case you failed to pay the fees by the deadline given to you the admission will automatically stand cancelled.
5. Hostel will be allocated on first come-first serve basis since we have limited number of seats in the hostel. In order to reserve your seat for hostel you will have to pay the full hostel fees at the earliest.
6. Second year fees must be paid before 15th March 2023 at respective campus.
 
D. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
1. The decision of the ITM Central Selection Process Governing Body is final.
2. You must confirm your attendance for centralized selection process by email or phone.
3. Based on your overall performance in the selection process, admission may be offered for one or more PGDM programmes and campus, taking into consideration your preference and availability of seats.
4. Please note that after you accept the Provisional Offer of Admission, no request will be entertained under any circumstances for change of campus and programme.
5. Please make arrangements to be at MP/PI venue as mentioned above. You have to make travel and stay arrangements on your own.
6. It is recommended that parents and applicants visit the various ITM centers, meet the local authorities and obtain firsthand information of the infrastructure at each center.
7. Applicants must prepare themselves to be at the MP/PI venue for at least 7 to 8 hours.
8 You should report at the venue as per the date and time specified in the call letter.
9 Your preference expressed in the application form will be used as a guide only.
E. RESOLUTION OF DISPUTES:
1. Any dispute arising out of matters stated in this document shall be subject to jurisdiction of competent authority in Mumbai only, irrespective of the programme or campus offered.
